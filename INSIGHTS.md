# Key Business Insights

## Revenue Analysis
- West region generated the highest overall sales.
- Sales showed a positive upward trend across the analysis period.

## Customer Analysis
- A small group of customers contributed a significant portion of revenue.
- Consumer segment generated the highest sales contribution.

## Product Analysis
- Technology category delivered strong revenue and profitability.
- Certain sub-categories consistently outperformed others.

## Profitability Analysis
- Higher discount levels negatively impacted profit margins.
- Profitability varied significantly across categories and regions.

# Business Recommendations

1. Reduce excessive discounting on low-margin products.
2. Strengthen retention strategies for high-value customers.
3. Invest further in high-performing categories.
4. Replicate successful regional strategies in underperforming markets.
