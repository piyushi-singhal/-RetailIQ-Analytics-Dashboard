1. What is the overall business performance in terms of revenue, profit, and order volume?
Objective: Measure the overall health and scale of the business.

SQL: (Your KPI query)

Insight Template: The business generated $X in sales and $Y in profit across Z unique orders, providing a high-level view of overall performance.



2. What is the company's profit margin?
Objective: Evaluate how effectively revenue is converted into profit.

SQL: (Profit Margin query)

Insight Template: The company achieved a profit margin of X%, indicating the efficiency of its pricing, cost management, and discount strategy.



3. Who are the highest-value customers?
Objective: Identify customers contributing the most revenue and profit.

SQL: (Top 10 Customers query)

Insight Template: A small group of customers contributes a significant share of revenue, highlighting opportunities for retention and loyalty programs.



4. Which products generate the highest sales?
Objective: Determine the best-selling products to support inventory and marketing decisions.

SQL: (Top Products query)

Insight Template: Top-performing products drive a disproportionate amount of sales and should be prioritized for stock availability and promotion.
